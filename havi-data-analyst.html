Turning Large Datasets into Actionable Patterns

Supply chain operations generate vast, heterogeneous data across sourcing, logistics, and inventory systems. The challenge is structuring it fast enough to be relevant to business decisions.

Identifying where to apply Lean and process improvement requires both quantitative analysis and the ability to communicate findings clearly to business stakeholders who don't live in spreadsheets.
